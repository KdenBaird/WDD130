Overview

Purpose

Have you ever had bad experiences with PT? Our company's mission is to offer exceptional service to our patients and to expand our business by opening clinics in Utah, Washington, and Oregon. What makes our company different is our friendly and supportive staff.

Audience

Our audience is any individual in need of Physical Therapy who would like superb treatment to offer a quick recovery.

Scheduling

This website makes scheduling an appointment easily accessible. To schedule an appointment, scroll down to the bottom of the page and click on "Schedule an Appointment." This link will redirect you to a separate page where you can request a certain therapist, and a time that works for you and your therapist.

FAQ

I'm sure some of you might have questions about our Physical Therapy business and how to navigate through our webpages. There is a "FAQ" link above the Site map portion of this webpage that will allow you to be redirected to a separate page with commonly asked questions and answers.
