FAQ

Here are some of the most frequently asked questions from our clients.

    What is the easiest way to book a reservation?

The easiest way to book a reservation is through our website. You can get there by going to our homepage then clicking on the link "Book a Reservation" which is located in the header. However, you're also able to book a reservation via phone call or walking in to our office.

    Am I able to cancel my reservation? Can I get a refund if I do?

Yes you are able to cancel your reservation. The easiest way to do this is to either email us or call our front office. Our contact information is in the section below. We have a strict 14-day no refund policy. If you were to cancel your reservation 14 days prior then we would refund your money. However, if you cancel your reservation within 14 days of it, then we wouldn't be able to refund you.

    What is your contact information, and what is the best way to get a hold of you?

The easiest way to communicate with us is through our email: DryOar@hotmail.com. Our phone number is 208.111.2000. Our fax number is 555.123.4567.
